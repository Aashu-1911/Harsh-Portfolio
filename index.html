Projects

College Healthcare Centre

I developed a College Healthcare Centre website to help students maintain their health and raise awareness about wellness. The website features key functionalities such as locating First Aid Kit stations across the campus and providing a detailed First Aid Kit manual for emergencies. Its primary function is to allow students to book free OPD appointments for health checkups, ensuring easy access to healthcare services. This project aims to promote health awareness and provide a convenient platform for students to prioritize their well-being.
